MXRG$TR is an interdisciplinary filmmaker and media artist based in Los Angeles, California.

In the 1990s his early works were almost exclusively projected 8mm/16mm film loops and large scale sound installations centered around unconscious cognitive processes.

By the early 2000s he had transitioned to working with electronic media namely Amiga 1000s, VHS and BetaMax systems to create video manipulations focussed on simulated worlds, impulse-triggers and control.

Since 2010 he has combined these two modes, traditional hands-on arts processes and code-based and algorithmic electronic filters, to mash culturally popular sounds and images into generated digital art. His most recent work features distortions of media hungry personalities and the networks that profit from their persistent, repetitive messaging cycles.

Ironically, he is also known internationally under a different moniker, as an established director in the music and advertising industries where his roster of clients includes DDB, Publicis/Mojo, Pepsi, Unilever, Logitech, Diageo, GoDaddy, Sony Entertainment, Atlantic & Universal Music.
